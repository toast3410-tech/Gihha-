Introduction
Who We Are

The Global Institute of Health & Humanitarian Advancement (GIHHA) is an impartial research and policy institute dedicated to advancing accountability, transparency, and equitable governance across health and humanitarian systems.

Operating at the intersection of public health, crisis response, and governance reform, GIHHA works to ensure that vulnerable populations — particularly those in fragile, conflict-affected, and underserved regions — receive fair, corruption-free, and high-quality care.
